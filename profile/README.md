### Libro — free and open source

[**Libro**](https://github.com/cerebrocybersolutions/libro) is the framework we run Cerebro on, released free under Apache 2.0. It gives your AI persistent memory, skills, and a department-brain structure on top of Claude Code. You own your memory, your index, and your rules. Bring your own Claude.

- Own it, don't rent it — your memory and index, on your machine
- Bring your own Claude — we never proxy your traffic
- Apache 2.0 — no lock-in, no subscription
